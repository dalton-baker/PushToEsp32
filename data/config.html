Easy Methods to Get Your Coordinates:

📱 iPhone - Compass App (Easiest!):
  1. Open the Compass app
  2. Your coordinates are shown at the bottom (e.g., "37.7749° N, 122.4194° W")
  3. Tap and hold on the coordinates to copy
  4. Paste below
🤖 Android - Drop a Pin:
  1. Open Google Maps
  2. Long-press anywhere on the map to drop a pin
  3. Coordinates appear at the bottom in the search box
  4. Tap the coordinates to select them, then copy
💡 Or Just Type Them:

You can also just estimate and type directly in the fields below. For astronomy, ±0.01° (about 1km) is fine!
